Messaging features in your apps

DLG provides a messaging solution from the frontend to the backend to integrate easily to communicate with your customers by your company's mobile and web applications.

Accessibility

Your company is always accessible. All of your messages are delivered to your devices. Even if you are offline, the messages you receive will not be lost...

DLG's ChatBot infrastructure, NLP and AI technologies allow you to provide automated response services to your customers

Accessibility

Your company's representatives or support team will always be accessible. Conduct your clients' transactions with enriched messaging, not over the phone. Increase customer satisfaction and commitment in this regard.

DLG iOS, Android and Web clients are always accessible from anywhere. You can start messaging on the web and then resume from your mobile devices. DLG distributes your messages to all of your devices for you. Customers can also communicate with you locally and in time on the device they want in the same way.

One-To-One or Group Messaging

Your customers can immediately reach the right representative or support person in your company. They can even make voice calls.

On the other hand, you can create customer groups and send information, campaigns and announcements to these groups collectively.

Notification

Any messages you send on the DLG will be notified to iOS and Android devices if your customer is not online. In this way, customers can instantly receive and view the message you send.

This feature allows the customer or your representatives to act quickly on messages that are delivered.

Send all types of messages

With DLG you can send text messages, pictures, photos, videos or location messages. If you feel it is hard to write, you can send it in voicemail.

You can send and receive all kinds of video and image files. You can also forward all your documents and files by DLG. In this way you can send and receive files to your customers.

In addition, there is no limit to the size of the file, image or video you send.

SDK, API and Integration

DLG provides you with SDKs that easily integrate into your company's mobile and web applications. With these SDKs, your applications become a messaging application easily and you start to write with your customers in your own applications.

You can also send messages to your customers by integrating them with your company's software and applications. For example, you can provide your company's CRM system announcements and messages to your customers.

Likewise, you can develop automated two-way services with your customers by integrating your applications with our BOTs we develop.

Automatic services with ChatBot

DLG offers you a BOT infrastructure. With this infrastructure, you can offer services that automatically respond to customers like a real person.

With Turkish NLP and AI technologies, you can create robots that understand and respond to speaking language.

You can automate your business with DLG robots and provide high-tech services to your customers, such as help services, frequently asked questions, and receiving information from your customers.
